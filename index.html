Expozia — Support

Expozia is a film light meter for iOS — precise metering, a manual flash calculator, ND compensation, and a logbook for every roll.

Contact

Questions, bugs, or feedback? Email me at expozia.app@gmail.com. I usually reply within a couple of days.

FAQ

Is there a subscription?
No. Expozia is a one-time purchase — every feature is included.

Which iPhones are supported?
iPhone running iOS 17 or later.

Does it work with any film?
Yes. Set your film's ISO and meter as usual; the logbook organizes shots by roll.
